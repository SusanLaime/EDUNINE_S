About EDUNINE

The IEEE World Engineering Education Conference (EDUNINE) will be held annually in various Latin American cities. This event, organized by the IEEE Education Society in collaboration with COPEC (Science and Research Organization) for IEEE Region 9 (Latin America), is a key conference in the IEEE Education Society's regional lineup. Other notable conferences in this series include FIE (Frontiers in Education), EDUCON (Global Engineering Education Conference), and TALE (Teaching Assessment and Learning in Engineering).
